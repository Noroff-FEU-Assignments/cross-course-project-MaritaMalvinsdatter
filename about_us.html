About us

Our first raincoat saw daylight for the first time back in 2003 and the rest is history. We believe that adventures should be for everyone and that's why we make affordable high-quality raincoats so you can go out and never have to worry about a rainy day. Our raincoats are made with recyled plastic from our oceans, and everytime you make a purchase we donate 2% of your buy to the non-profit organization The Ocean Cleanup.

All raincoats comes with a 5 year warranty, and we will also help you with a repair if an accident happens and it's not covered by the warranty. Get in touch with us and we'll help you.
